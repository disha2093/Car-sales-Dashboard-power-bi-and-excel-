# Car-sales-Dashboard-power-bi
Transforming Data into Drive: My Newest Car Sales Analytics Dashboard! 🚗📊
I’m excited to share a project I’ve been working on: a comprehensive Car Sales Performance Dashboard built entirely in Power BI.
In the fast-paced automotive industry, understanding the numbers is the key to staying ahead. This dashboard provides a 360-degree view of sales performance, allowing for data-driven decisions at a glance.
Key Insights & Features:
KPI Tracking: Real-time monitoring of YTD Total Sales ($371.2M), Average Price ($28.0K), and total units sold (13.3K).
Trend Analysis: A weekly sales trend line to identify seasonal peaks and performance fluctuations.
Inventory Breakdown: Visualizing sales by Body Style (SUV, Hatchback, Sedan) and Color to understand consumer preferences.
Geographical Intelligence: An interactive map tracking car sales across major dealer regions like Austin, Janesville, and Scottsdale.
Competitive Benchmarking: A "Company Wise Sales Trend" table that deep-dives into performance metrics by brand, including Toyota, Volkswagen, and Volvo.
This project focuses on turning raw data into actionable insights through intuitive UI/UX design and advanced data modeling.
